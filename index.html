CESS Campus Ambassador


Join our team and become a CESS Capmus Ambassador. We want to create a collaborative environment between our society and delegates of respective institutions. If you get selected as CESS Campus Ambassador of your institution, we have valuable perks, goodies, amazing discounts and much more on our events and workshops to offer you.

PERKS OF BEING CESS CAMPUS AMBASSADOR :

1. You can attend all minor events and workshops for FREE.
2. Exposure towards your working domain will be enhanced and hence creating life time opportunities for your career.
3. You will be given extra 50% discount on all events with registration more than Rs.100.
4. You and your institution will be awarded with positive media support.
5. We will make sure to encourage your support in our event domain.
6. You will get a mention of honour on Cess Website.
7. You will be honored with Campus Ambassador Certification affiliated by our organization.

ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES OF CESS CAMPUS AMBASSADOR :

To promote our events and workshops in your campus and redirect any event related query to us. Represent us in your college. We are happy to help anytime.
